Ella to Nuwara Eliya — Scenic Tea Route by Motorcycle

By RidexHub • Sep 10, 2025 • 10 min read
Share

Start early for misty tea-estate views and cool temperatures. This route climbs through winding passes and offers multiple viewpoints and waterfall stops.

Why ride this route

The road between Ella and Nuwara Eliya showcases Sri Lanka's hill country — winding tarmac, tea gardens, and charming towns. It's ideal for experienced riders and those comfortable with elevation changes.

Suggested itinerary

  1. Ella — Little Adam’s Peak: quick morning stretch
  2. Ravana Falls — scenic stop and breakfast
  3. Tea factory visit near Nuwara Eliya

Safety notes

Check brakes and tyre pressure for mountain rides. Carry rain protection and be mindful of sudden fog in the mornings.

Local tips

Stop for tea and eat at local kiosks — great way to meet locals and try fresh hoppers.
